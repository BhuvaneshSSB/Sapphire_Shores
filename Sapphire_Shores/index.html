ROOMS

--------------------------------------------

JUNIOR SUITE

The Junior Suite offers a spacious and elegant retreat perfect for both business and leisure travelers. This suite features a comfortable king-sized bed, a stylish partition separating the bedroom from the living area, and a well-appointed dining space. Modern amenities include a flat-screen TV, a coffee maker, and high-speed internet access. The contemporary decor and thoughtful design ensure a relaxing and enjoyable stay.

PENTHOUSE SUITE

Our luxurious Penthouse Suite is designed for guests seeking the ultimate in comfort and sophistication. Located on the top floor, this suite boasts floor-to-ceiling windows with stunning city views, a cozy fireplace, and plush seating areas adorned with vibrant furnishings. The suite also includes a separate bedroom with a king-sized bed, a spacious living room, and a dining area ideal for entertaining. Enjoy premium amenities such as a large flat-screen TV, high-speed internet, and an in-suite coffee maker.

PRESIDENTIAL SUITE

Experience unparalleled luxury in our Presidential Suite, the epitome of elegance and style. This expansive suite offers breathtaking views of the city and ocean through its floor-to-ceiling windows. The sophisticated living area features contemporary furnishings, a fireplace, and a large flat-screen TV. The suite includes a master bedroom with a king-sized bed, a second bedroom for guests, a dining area, and a fully equipped kitchen. Additional amenities include high-speed internet access, a coffee maker, and exclusive access to premium services. Perfect for discerning travelers, the Presidential Suite ensures a memorable stay.
